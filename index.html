About Auticare

Auticare An XR-AI based Assistive Technology tool

An XR-AI based Assistive Technology tool and a learning platform for Autism Spectrum Disorder(ASD) and related neurodevelopmental conditions. Auticare uses virtual reality scenarios based on ABA therapy modules to rehabilitate autistic children in major skill components related to
- Cognitive Aspects
- Physical Aspects
- Sensory Aspects
- Behavioural Aspects
- Social Aspects
- Community Based Aspects
- Self-Care Aspects

Virtual Scenarios and Modules in Auticare aims to improve the above deficit skills related to cognitive, sensory, physical, behavioural, social, self-care and community based aspects, also helps to identify hidden gifted abilities/capabilities among autistic children.

Our platform has more than 20 virtual environments and scenes specially designed to easily treat dozens of pathologies. The therapist can use multiple therapeutic techniques (psychoeducation, gradual exposure, systematic desensitization, relaxation, mindfulness) to work with the patients.
